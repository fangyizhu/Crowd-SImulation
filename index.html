Crowd Simulation

(Runnable application is in repository.)

(10) Allow user to control some characters or obstacles in real time, simulated agents should replan or react dynamically to the user. Clicking to switch on/off followMouse mode.

Agents avoid each other and be attracted to the mouse successfully at most of the time. But because obstacles are randomly generated, occasionally agents get 'stuck' between obstacles and obstacles, or obstacles and walls.
